Chicken Taco Lasagna

Description

the best chicken lasagna youll ever have in your life

Ingredients

      1. Four boneless skinless chicken breasts cut into 1/4 strips
      2. A-third Cup lime Juice
      3. One Cup Sour cream
      4. Eight Ounce Package lasagna noodles
      5. One Cup Salsa
      6. One and a half cups of mozerrella cheese

Steps

    Step 1: Combine chicken, lime juice, and taco seasoning in a large bowl. Stir to make sure chicken is evenly covered
    Step 2: Cook chicken mixture over medium heat until firm, about 15 minutes.
    Step 3: Mix 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, sour cream, and salsa in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
    Step 5: Put a few spoonfuls of the sour cream mixture in the bottom of a 9x9-inch baking dish. Layer noodles and chicken on top. Cover with remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
    Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven until noodles are tender and cheese is melted, about 1 hour.
